Our Love Story

How We Met

Our paths crossed on a rainy afternoon at the Riverside Café. Emma was reading her favorite novel, and James accidentally spilled his coffee nearby. What started as an awkward apology turned into hours of conversation and laughter.

Three years of adventures, challenges, and growth later, we're ready to begin our greatest journey yet—marriage.

The Proposal

After months of secret planning, James proposed during our trip to the coast. With the sunset painting the sky in shades of pink and orange, he got down on one knee on the same beach where we had our first vacation.

Through happy tears, Emma said "yes," and we celebrated under the stars, dreaming of our future together.
